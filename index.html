Let your phone give you a hand

Third Hand on the iPhone/iPad lets you keep an overview of your clipboard history of your Mac. With a single tap you can paste any clipboard item. Ideal when writing text or working on images: You will never again have to copy the same thing twice.

How it works

Third Hand keeps an eye out for any new items on the clipboard on your Mac and adds them in a list that is directly visible on your iPhone/iPad. Tapping any item in the list will paste it in the application you have open on your Mac.

Don't worry, your data is safe: data is encrypted and sent only between your Mac and your iPhone/iPad. Deleting a clipboard item with sensitive data from the list is as simple as a simple swipe.
